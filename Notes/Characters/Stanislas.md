# Strong antagonist guy

- Name: Stanislas
- Originally a physics researcher at CERN
- Looks physically strong (tall and built) but kind of ghastly, similar to RoA vibes
- Lone wolf, lives in the paris catacombs where his frozen opponents are left as trophies
- Driven mad by his power and ego, believes accumulating mystic power will make him a god feared by all
- Wants to find the original god remnants in order to study them and acquire the power for himself
  - Has found a copy of John Doe's work
  - Used it to determine the position of where the god remnants are in the world

## Powers

- Ice powers, leaves people frozen
- Heat manipulation? goes with the ice
